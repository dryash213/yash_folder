Class 12 Biology Sample Paper
Section A: (1 mark each)
What is photosynthesis?
Define the term 'gene.'
What is the function of ribosomes?
Name the process by which DNA is copied.
What is the primary role of chlorophyll?
Define biodiversity.
Section B: (2 marks each)
Describe the structure of a plant cell.
Explain the process of cellular respiration.
What are the main components of blood?
Describe the different types of RNA.
What is the significance of the cell cycle?
Explain the concept of natural selection.
Section C: (3 marks each)
Discuss the importance of photosynthesis in the ecosystem.
Explain the role of enzymes in biochemical reactions.
Describe the process of protein synthesis.
Discuss the impact of human activities on the environment.
Explain the significance of the theory of evolution.
Describe the structure and function of the human heart.
Explain the process of fertilization in flowering plants.
Discuss the significance of genetic engineering.
Describe the process of mitosis and its importance.
Explain the role of hormones in human physiology.
Section D: (4 marks each)
Describe the different types of ecological pyramids and their significance.
Explain the concept of homeostasis in living organisms.
Discuss the structure and function of the human digestive system.
Explain the significance of biodiversity conservation.
Describe the process of excretion in humans.
Discuss the role of biotechnology in agriculture.
Explain the structure and function of the nervous system.
Describe the process of vaccination and its importance in public health.
